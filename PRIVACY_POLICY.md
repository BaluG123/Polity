## Introduction

Welcome to TargetPolity ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application TargetPolity (the "App"). This policy applies to all users of our App.

By using TargetPolity, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use our App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information**: Email address, display name, and profile picture (when you sign in with Google)
- **Authentication Data**: User ID and authentication tokens provided by Google Sign-In
- **Profile Information**: Any additional information you choose to provide in your user profile

### Usage Information
We automatically collect certain information about your use of the App:

- **Quiz Performance**: Your quiz scores, completion times, difficulty levels attempted
- **Study Progress**: Topics studied, time spent on different sections, learning streaks
- **App Usage**: Features accessed, session duration, frequency of use
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, app version
- **Technical Data**: IP address, device identifiers, crash reports, performance metrics

### Content Interaction
- **Bookmarks**: Articles, cases, and topics you bookmark for later reference
- **Study History**: Your progress through constitutional topics and historical events
- **Leaderboard Data**: Your ranking, points earned, and competitive performance

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Core App Functionality
- **User Authentication**: To create and manage your account securely
- **Progress Tracking**: To save and sync your learning progress across devices
- **Quiz System**: To provide personalized quiz experiences and track performance
- **Leaderboard**: To display rankings and competitive features
- **Content Personalization**: To recommend relevant study materials

### App Improvement
- **Analytics**: To understand how users interact with our App and improve user experience
- **Performance Monitoring**: To identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
- **Feature Development**: To develop new features based on user behavior and feedback

### Communication
- **Notifications**: To send study reminders and important app updates (with your consent)
- **Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

- **Right to Non-Discrimination**: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights
